Why I made this? ╦ ╦┬ ┬┬ ┬ ╦ ┌┬┐┌─┐┌┬┐┌─┐ ┌┬┐┬ ┬┬┌─┐┌─┐ ║║║├─┤└┬┘ ║ │││├─┤ ││├┤ │ ├─┤│└─┐ ┌┘ ╚╩╝┴ ┴ ┴ ╩ ┴ ┴┴ ┴─┴┘└─┘ ┴ ┴ ┴┴└─┘ o

I do love using ASCII diagrams for demonstrating the workflow of something, especially for system design. It is also very easy to share and edit just with a simple edit text like NotePad. Moreover, ASCII's chart is the only embeddable diagram within the code, which is my everyday job.

Before, I tried several tools and felt acceptable with Textik. However, Textik's shapes do not look good to me over time. Then I found out a macOS's native app, Monodraw. Unfortunately, it's not free. I didn't want to pay, so, I code. Besides, some friends of mine said that they were not allowed to install Monodraw as the policy from their companies, therefore, it's a good chance for developing something that works on the browser rather than a native app.

At the time I started this project, I almost had no experience with the web app. It took me a while for quite standardized source code.
Kotlin/JS is used for writing the control logic and SASS for styling.
