╦ ╦┬ ┬┬ ┬ ╦ ┌┬┐┌─┐┌┬┐┌─┐ ┌┬┐┬ ┬┬┌─┐┌─┐ ║║║├─┤└┬┘ ║ │││├─┤ ││├┤ │ ├─┤│└─┐ ┌┘ ╚╩╝┴ ┴ ┴ ╩ ┴ ┴┴ ┴─┴┘└─┘ ┴ ┴ ┴┴└─┘ o Why I made this? I do love using ASCII diagrams for demonstrating the workflow of something, especially for system design. It is also very easy to share and edit just with a simple edit text like NotePad. Moreover, ASCII's chart is the only embeddable diagram within the code, which is my everyday job. Before, I tried several tools and felt acceptable with Textik. However, Textik's shapes do not look good to me. Then I found out a macOS's native app, Monodraw. Unfortunately, it's not free. I didn't want to pay, so, I code. Besides, some friends of mine said that they were not allowed to install Monodraw as the policy from their companies, therefore, it's a good chance for developing something that works on the browser rather than a native app.
